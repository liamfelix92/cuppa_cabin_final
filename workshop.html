Which workshop are you interested in?

Learn a new skill or make something beautiful

We run a number of exciting workshops at The Cuppa Cabin and are always offering new options, take a look at what we're offering this month:

Needle Felting - Toadstool

Learn the art of Needle Felting with Lynne from So To Felt in the relaxed setting of Greenvalley. £25 per person. Each workshop lasts approximately 2 hours (plus coffee break) and includes tuition, materials and a drink from the Cuppa Cabin. Suitable for children over 10, but must be supervised by an adult.

Willow Weaving - Sunflowers

Spend a day with Richard from Windrush Willow learning the skills needed to create Decorations for your garden. £75 per person. All materials and tools included in price; as well as drinks throughout the day from the Cuppa Cabin. Food available to purchase from the Cuppa Cabin but feel free to bring a packed lunch.

Preloved Jewellery

Learn simple stringing techniques, remove broken clasps and learn how to add or change fastenings to make your preloved jewellery wearable again. £25 per person. Students will need to bring their old and broken jewellery to the workshop. Tools and materials included to use on the day. A range of beads will be available to add to your work but you can bring your own.

Spanglewangle Crafty Sessions

A day of arts and crafts just for the children! Fab fun with fake fur, come make tails, ears & more. Or whatever your little one fancies! £6.50 per child, all materials included.
